About the Studio

Precision Built. Factory Direct.

Chris Caruso started this business with a simple conviction: if you control every stage of the process, you never have to compromise on quality. That means designing, manufacturing and installing every cabinet ourselves - from our own factory in Direk, right through to the final fitting at your property.

Over 15 years and hundreds of projects later, that philosophy hasn't changed. Whether it's a full kitchen renovation for a homeowner in the northern suburbs or a commercial fitout for a builder in the CBD, every job gets the same meticulous standard. No outsourcing. No shortcuts. No exceptions.
