Why Trade Partners Choose Us

One partner, from drawing to install.

We run our own custom cabinetry warehouse - which means you get one team that designs nothing away, outsources nothing, and answers for everything. Kitchens, wardrobes, vanities, shelving, display units, reception desks, bar fitouts, retail joinery - if you can draw it, we can build it. Bring us the vision; we'll craft every piece in-house, to spec, on schedule. No coordinating three suppliers. No defects landing on your site. No surprises on handover.

The Easy Guide

How the hand-off works

Three steps. You stay in control of the design - we take care of making it real.

01

Send Your Plans

Email us your drawings, CAD files, mood boards or a rough sketch. We review the spec and confirm scope, lead time and pricing - fast.

02

We Manufacture It

Every cabinet is built to the millimetre in our Adelaide factory - your finishes, your hardware, your spec. One team, fully accountable, zero outsourcing.

03

Delivered To Site

We deliver and install to your build schedule, coordinating directly with your trades - clean, on time, and exactly as drawn. Your client is impressed; you look good.
