Our Story

Built by hand, start to finish.

Chris Caruso started this business with a simple conviction: if you control every stage of the process, you never have to compromise on quality. That means designing, manufacturing and installing every cabinet ourselves - from our own factory in Direk, right through to the final fitting at your property.

Over 15 years and hundreds of projects later, that philosophy hasn't changed. Every job gets the same meticulous standard. No outsourcing. No shortcuts. No exceptions - whether it's a single vanity or a full commercial fitout.
